# Resources

External materials worth knowing about. These are reference links, not things to copy into this vault — don't vendor other people's work unless you have a specific reason.

## Spanish

**Courses and input**
- [Language Transfer — Complete Spanish](https://www.languagetransfer.org/complete-spanish) — free audio course, also available as a podcast
- [Dreaming Spanish](https://www.dreamingspanish.com/) — comprehensible input video library (free and paid tiers; podcast feed is free)
- [SpanishPod101](https://www.spanishpod101.com/) — well-structured lessons, free account gives you a lot

**Grammar references**
- [StudySpanish.com](https://studyspanish.com/) — old but solid reference for verb tenses and the tricky bits
- [SpanishDict](https://www.spanishdict.com/) — best free dictionary, includes conjugation tables

**Community Obsidian vaults**
- Search GitHub for `spanish obsidian vault` — a few exist but they tend to be personal and half-finished. Don't adopt wholesale. Skim for ideas on structure if you want.

## French

**Courses and input**
- [Coffee Break French](https://coffeebreaklanguages.com/coffeebreakfrench/) — structured lessons, podcast feed is free
- [InnerFrench](https://innerfrench.com/) — intermediate comprehensible input by Hugo
- [Français Authentique](https://www.francaisauthentique.com/) — similar level to InnerFrench, different register
- [News in Slow French](https://www.newsinslowfrench.com/) — Friday-checkpoint podcast

**Grammar references**
- [Lawless French](https://www.lawlessfrench.com/) — Laura K. Lawless's grammar site, most comprehensive free reference online
- [Le Conjugueur](https://leconjugueur.lefigaro.fr/) — conjugation tables

## Japanese

**Courses and input**
- [Tofugu](https://www.tofugu.com/) — their free articles are excellent, WaniKani is their paid kanji SRS
- [Nihongo con Teppei](https://nihongoconteppei.com/) — podcast for ambient listening
- [Cure Dolly](https://www.youtube.com/@organicjapanesewithcuredolly6039) — unusual but deeply clarifying grammar explanations on YouTube

**Once you're active (week 7+)**
- [Genki I + II textbooks](https://genki3.japantimes.co.jp/en/) — still the gold standard for structured beginner Japanese
- [Bunpro](https://bunpro.jp/) — grammar SRS; complements vocab SRS nicely
