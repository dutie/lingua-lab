# éclair

éclair::flash of lightning; also: eclair (pastry) #sr/fr

## Examples
- *Un éclair a illuminé le ciel.* — A flash of lightning lit up the sky.
- *Un éclair au chocolat* — a chocolate eclair.

## Related
- *foudre* — the actual lightning bolt, the striking force
- *tonnerre* — thunder
- *orage* — thunderstorm

## Notes

French distinguishes *éclair* (the visible flash) from *foudre* (the destructive bolt). *La foudre est tombée* = lightning struck. *Un éclair* = "a flash." English and Spanish both mostly collapse this.

## Spanish bridge

ES: *relámpago* (flash) / *rayo* (bolt). Same two-way split, roughly aligned: *éclair ≈ relámpago*, *foudre ≈ rayo*. French and Spanish agree here; English is the odd one out.
