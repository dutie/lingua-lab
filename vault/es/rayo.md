# rayo

rayo::ray / lightning bolt #sr/es

## Examples
- *Un rayo cayó cerca de la casa.* — A lightning bolt struck near the house.
- *Un rayo de sol.* — A ray of sunlight.
- *¡Eres un rayo!* — You're lightning-fast!

## Related
- *relámpago* — the flash (the light itself, not the bolt)
- *trueno* — thunder

## French bridge

FR: *rayon* (ray of light, spoke of a wheel). Note the divergence: Spanish *rayo* covers both "lightning bolt" and "ray"; French *rayon* only covers "ray" — for lightning French uses *éclair* or *foudre*.

See [[../concepts/Lightning]].
