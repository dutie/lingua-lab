# dejar

dejar::to leave / to let / to allow; dejar de + inf = to stop doing #sr/es

## Examples
- *Déjame en paz.* — Leave me alone.
- *Dejé de fumar.* — I stopped smoking.
- *¿Me dejas tu boli?* — Can I borrow your pen?

## Notes

High-frequency. Multi-meaning. Don't confuse with *salir* (to leave a place): *salir de casa* vs *dejar la casa*.

## French bridge

FR cognate: *laisser* (to leave, to let). Same semantic range — *laisse-moi tranquille* maps directly to *déjame en paz*. But the "stop doing" sense uses *arrêter de* in French, not *laisser de*.
