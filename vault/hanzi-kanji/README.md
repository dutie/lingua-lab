# Hanzi-Kanji

**Dormant until week 13+.** This folder exists to hold *cross-linguistic* character studies for Chinese hanzi + Japanese kanji that share the same character (most of them, since Japanese adopted Chinese characters).

A note in here is about the character itself — its CN reading (pinyin), JP on'yomi and kun'yomi, shared meaning, divergent compounds. The character's the anchor, the readings diverge.

Empty by design until you start Chinese around week 13.
