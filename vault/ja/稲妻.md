# 稲妻

稲妻::いなずま — lightning (visible flash, literary) #sr/ja

## Examples
- *稲妻が光った。* — Lightning flashed.

## Related
- 雷 (kaminari) — thunder / thunder-and-lightning as weather. Common daily word.
- 閃光 (senkō) — flash of light. Technical/formal.

## Notes

Literary register. For everyday "it's lightning," 雷 is more common. The kanji breakdown: 稲 = rice plant, 妻 = wife. Etymologically "rice's wife," from a folk belief that lightning fertilized rice fields. Don't try to derive meaning from the kanji; learn as a unit.
