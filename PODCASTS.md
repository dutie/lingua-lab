# Podcasts — what to subscribe to in Audiobookshelf

In ABS: left sidebar → Podcasts library → **+** → **Find Podcast** tab → search by name → pick the top match → enable auto-download.

RSS URLs rot over time. Searching by name via iTunes is more reliable than hardcoding feed URLs — when a podcast migrates hosts, iTunes updates; static URLs don't.

## Spanish (primary on Mon/Wed/Fri)

- **Language Transfer — Complete Spanish**
  Guided-reasoning grammar course. Single best resource to build Spanish intuition from a Romance-language-speaker base. Non-negotiable for weeks 1–6.

- **Dreaming Spanish Podcast** ("Chats in Beginner Spanish")
  Comprehensible input. Two teachers chat; you follow along without looking anything up.

- **News in Slow Spanish**
  Friday-commute checkpoint. Pick the Spain or Latino variant based on which accent you'd rather internalize — no wrong answer. Spain = closer to standard European, matches your French exposure. Latino = what you'll hear in most Spanish media.

- **Españolistos**
  Conversational Spanish between a Colombian and an American. Add this when Dreaming Spanish starts feeling too slow (probably week 3–4).

## French (primary on Tue/Thu)

- **Coffee Break French**
  Beginner-friendly, structured lessons. Since your French is already past zero, use early episodes as revision and jump ahead when it feels too slow.

- **InnerFrench**
  Intermediate comprehensible input. Hugo speaks at near-native speed but clearly, on topics like politics, tech, culture. The French equivalent of Dreaming Spanish but assumes more.

- **News in Slow French**
  Optional Friday alternative if News in Slow Spanish gets repetitive. Same format, same company.

## Japanese (ambient only — 17:00 break)

Listen passively. No notes, no lookups. Goal is ear training.

- **Nihongo con Teppei (for beginners)**
  The canonical beginner input podcast. Slow, clear, daily-life topics.

- **Japanese with Shun**
  Similar level, different voice. Rotate to avoid adapting too narrowly to one speaker's pronunciation.

## After you subscribe

In ABS, set per-podcast options:
- Auto-download: on
- Keep at most 10 episodes (prevents your server from hoarding 600 episodes of LT you'll never get to)
- Mark LT episodes as finished in order — it's a course, not a drop-in podcast, so skipping around breaks the progression
